# Excel-Sales-Performance-Metrics-Dashboard
Online Course Sales Analysis Project (Excel / Data Analysis)
Project Overview

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of an online course sales dataset, covering course enrollments, fees, advertising channels, training models, training levels, and sales team performance. The primary goal was to identify key drivers of sales, course popularity, and advertising effectiveness to support strategic business decisions for the online education company.

##Dataset & Scope

The dataset included the following information for each course:

Fee status and total fees paid

Advertising channel

Enrolled courses per month

Number of phone calls and average call duration

Training models and training levels

Area code, sales team, and consultant

##Key Insights

Advertising Channels: Television ads generated the highest number of leads, followed by company websites and Facebook pages.

Training Models Popularity: GK and BE models were the most preferred among learners.

Training Levels Popularity: Foundation Level 5 and Foundation Level 3 attracted the most enrollments.

Enrollment Trends: September recorded the highest number of enrolled courses.

Financial Performance: Total fees collected reached EGP 200,000,000.

Operational Insights: The average number of phone calls per course was 3, indicating efficient lead follow-up.

##Business Recommendations

Marketing Strategy: Continue investment in television advertising to maximize reach and lead generation.

Course Offering Strategy: Focus on GK and BE training models to match learner demand.

Level-Specific Courses: Maintain Foundation Level 5 and 3 courses to satisfy learners across different expertise levels.

Operational Efficiency: Monitor call activities to optimize consultant efforts and improve conversion rates.

##Tools & Techniques Used

Excel: For data cleaning, visualization, and dashboard creation.

Descriptive Analysis: Aggregation of enrollments, fees, and call metrics.

Trend Analysis: Monthly enrollment trends and popular course tracking.

Business Insights: Translating analysis into actionable recommendations for marketing, course planning, and operations.

##Outcome

This analysis provides a data-driven foundation for enhancing marketing strategies, optimizing course offerings, and increasing overall revenue for the online education company. It equips stakeholders with actionable insights to improve learner engagement and sales performance.
